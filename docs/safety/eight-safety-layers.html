The Eight Safety Layers

Aviation safety is not linear. It is layered.
Each layer serves as a performance-controlled defence.
When monitoring, adaptation, or insulation fails across layers, risk accumulates.
◔

Human Factors

Cognition, authority gradients, workload, and communication under operational pressure.

📖

Training &
Competency

Technical proficiency and non-technical skills shaped by structured learning systems.

⚙

SOP & Operational
Design

Procedure clarity, checklist architecture, and phraseology precision.

↯

Maintenance &
Engineering

Mechanical integrity and infrastructure resilience under normal and surge conditions.

◜

Organizational
Leadership

Governance structures, safety culture, and operational decision architecture.

◷

Safety Assurance

Safety Assurance functions as the monitoring and performance verification mechanism acting across the system.

Technology & Design

Surveillance systems, automation logic, and redundancy layers.
