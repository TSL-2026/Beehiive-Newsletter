The Swiss Cheese at LaGuardia

On February 6, 2025, a Delta regional jet and an American Airlines aircraft collided on a runway at LaGuardia Airport. Seventeen people were injured. The investigation is ongoing — but the causal chain is already familiar: multiple small failures aligning in sequence. This edition examines how James Reason's Swiss Cheese Model applies here, and why the same latent conditions exist in Nepal's high-altitude operations where margin for error is even thinner.
